Vishwesh Venkatraman

I am an Associate Principal Scientist at Lonza. My research so far has focused on the design and development of tools for the computer-aided design of materials used in solar cells, batteries, organic electronics and for drug development. As part of these efforts I use a combination of quantum chemistry and machine learning.
